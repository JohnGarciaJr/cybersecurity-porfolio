# Risk Register Project

This folder contains a sample enterprise risk register and a detailed risk assessment demonstrating practical risk management skills. These artifacts reflect common processes used in cybersecurity governance programs to identify, evaluate, and treat risks.

## Contents
- **Risk-Register.md** – A structured risk register with likelihood, impact, controls, and treatment plans.
- **Sample-Risk-Assessment.md** – A detailed assessment of a single risk, including threats, vulnerabilities, and recommended mitigation steps.
- **(Optional) Heat Map** – A visual representation of risk severity.

These documents are aligned with industry frameworks, such as NIST CSF, ISO 27005, and NIST 800-30.
