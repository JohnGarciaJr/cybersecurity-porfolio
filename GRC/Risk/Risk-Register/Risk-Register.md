# Enterprise Risk Register  
Version: 1.0  
Owner: Information Security Governance  
Last Updated: YYYY-MM-DD  

This risk register provides a structured view of key cybersecurity and operational risks, including likelihood, impact, existing controls, and recommended treatment plans.

| Risk ID | Risk Description | Likelihood | Impact | Inherent Risk | Existing Controls | Residual Risk | Risk Owner | Treatment Plan |
|--------|------------------|------------|--------|----------------|-------------------|----------------|------------|----------------|
| R-001 | Unauthorized access to sensitive data due to weak access controls | Medium | High | High | MFA, access reviews, RBAC | Medium | IT Security | Strengthen access reviews, implement JIT access |
| R-002 | Data loss due to insufficient backup processes | Low | High | Medium | Daily backups, cloud redundancy | Low | Infrastructure | Test restore procedures quarterly |
| R-003 | Phishing attack leading to credential compromise | High | Medium | High | Email filtering, awareness training | Medium | Security Awareness | Expand phishing simulations |
| R-004 | Misconfigured cloud resources exposing data publicly | Medium | High | High | CSPM scanning, IaC templates | Medium | Cloud Engineering | Enforce automated configuration baselines |
| R-005 | Third-party vendor breach impacting shared data | Medium | High | High | Vendor risk assessments, contracts | Medium | Procurement | Increase frequency of vendor reviews |
