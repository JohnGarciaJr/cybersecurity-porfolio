# Sample Risk Assessment  
Risk ID: R-001  
Risk: Unauthorized access to sensitive data due to weak access controls  
Assessment Date: YYYY-MM-DD  
Assessor: John (Information Security Governance)

## 1. Risk Description  
Weak or inconsistent access controls may allow unauthorized users to access sensitive or regulated data, therefore, resulting in data exposure, compliance violations, or operational disruption.

## 2. Assets Affected  
- Identity and Access Management (IAM) systems  
- HR data  
- Customer data  
- Internal applications  

## 3. Threats  
- Compromised credentials  
- Excessive permissions  
- Lack of access reviews  
- Insider misuse  

## 4. Vulnerabilities  
- No automated access review process  
- Inconsistent role definitions  
- Manual provisioning errors  

## 5. Likelihood  
**Medium** – Access issues occur periodically, and manual processes increase risk.

## 6. Impact  
**High** – Unauthorized access to sensitive data could result in regulatory penalties and reputational damage.

## 7. Inherent Risk  
**High**

## 8. Existing Controls  
- Multi-factor authentication  
- Role-based access control  
- Manual quarterly access reviews  

## 9. Residual Risk  
**Medium**

## 10. Recommended Treatment  
- Implement automated access review workflows  
- Define standardized roles and entitlements  
- Introduce just-in-time (JIT) access for privileged accounts  

## 11. Treatment Owner  
IT Security

## 12. Review Cycle  
Quarterly
